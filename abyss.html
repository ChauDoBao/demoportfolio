CALL OF THE ABYSS

Illustrator, Animator

“Call of the Abyss” is the website for a fictional underwater fundraising music concert. This website aims to provide users with information and the opportunity not only to participate in this concert but also to contribute to a friendly ecosystem through buying merchandise and tickets. Therefore, the concert can be held successfully and the profits can be used to conserve the ocean by planting corals. This project got an HD (High Distinction) mark and was shown on the RMIT Showcase Website.

Before development from our team, the idea was originally mine so I participated deeply in refining the idea. Continuing my role from the last project, I was mainly in charge of illustrating assets such as the merch section, the tickets, the seat map, the location map, etc… I also created the title typography and animation. In terms of UX/UI design, I built the early mid-fidelity version of the website.
