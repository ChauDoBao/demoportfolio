WATER PIECE

Illustrator, Animator

This is an individual 2D Motion Graphics project to interpret a Haiku poem named “Water Piece" from “Ono Yoko's Grapefruit" by Ono Yoko . About the book, the author said that the book was a cure for her, a place for her to release her "madness."

Following that, in my motion graphic, I want to have things that are absurd, do not exist in reality, and are beyond normal sensory perception, but they must still be peaceful and poetic. I believe the scenery should be serene enough to make people forget the cruel reality of life.

My interpretation of the poem “Water Piece” is that this poem is aimed at appreciating the eternal beauty of nature, focusing on the two main elements: water and the moon . These two objects may appear unrelated in some ways, but they share similar characteristics that represent the never-ending cycle of life. Therefore, I thought I should show this connection clearly in my work by creating “a loop" when the moon turns into water and the other way around.
