WACKY CHICKY

Illustrator, Animator

“Wacky Chicky” is an interactive digital infographic that walks users through the process of building a farm in seven steps. With this project, we aim to provide an educational and entertaining experience for users with a variety of interactions. The illustrations employ a flat shading style and isometric perspective art with a farm background to make two-dimensional figures look more like three-dimensional ones. In addition, the layout simulates the game interface with a general map where players’ activities take place.

In this project, I worked as one of the main illustrators. My illustration includes all of the background scenes, the typography for the title and some foreground elements. Besides, I took charge of making animation that helped create a more dynamic infographic. I also deeply participated in the ideation process.

This project got an HD (High Distinction) mark and was shown on the RMIT Showcase Website.
